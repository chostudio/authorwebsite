Electricity

A clever student finds himself admitted into a prestigious academy for elemental powers. There's only one problem: Zapp has no idea how to unlock his power. He'll have to rely on his wits to survive in the BadgeBattle, an annual schoolwide battle royale match where the top students compete for a coveted prize. With a fighting tournament in lieu of finals, a campus filled with secret passageways, and a graduation requirement of defeating a professor from each of the four Elemental Houses, Elementum Academy is a one-of-a-kind institution. But when Zapp uncovers a dark secret that threatens to overthrow the academy, he'll have to ask himself what it really means to have powers.

About the Author

Chris Ho is a computer science major and aspires to become a software engineer. Other than writing books, his interests include video game development, 3D modeling, trivia, and trying to become ambidextrous. His first novel is Elementum Academy: Electricity.
